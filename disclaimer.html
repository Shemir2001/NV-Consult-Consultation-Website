NVConsult.eu is an immigration information and consultancy platform. Our services are designed to help individuals understand immigration pathways, visa options, job opportunities, and relocation processes in Europe.

Our consultants provide general information, guidance, and strategic direction based on publicly available immigration rules, official government sources, and professional experience. This is distinct from legal advice, which requires a qualified solicitor or immigration lawyer who is registered and authorised to practise law.

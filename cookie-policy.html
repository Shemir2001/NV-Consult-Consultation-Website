Cookie Policy

Effective date: April 2025

1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognise your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences or actions.

Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide reporting information to site owners. Some cookies are essential for the website to function; others are optional and require your consent.

2 How We Use Cookies

NVConsult.eu uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential operation: to ensure the website loads correctly and your session is maintained.
  • Analytics: to understand how visitors navigate our site so we can improve content and structure.
  • Marketing: to measure the effectiveness of our Facebook ads and Google campaigns (only with your consent).

4 Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services that appear on our website. We do not control these cookies. The relevant third parties and their privacy policies are:

  • Google Analytics: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Meta (Facebook Pixel): facebook.com/privacy/policy
  • Cal.com (booking tool): cal.com/privacy

5 Your Choices: How to Control Cookies

Cookie consent banner: When you first visit NVConsult.eu, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can accept all cookies or accept only essential cookies. You can change your choice at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

You may also control cookies directly through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect how the website functions.

Browser-specific guides for managing cookies:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari (mobile & desktop): Settings > Safari > Privacy & Security
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking specifically, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

To opt out of Facebook interest-based advertising: facebook.com/adpreferences

6 Cookie Consent Banner

Our cookie consent banner reads: "We use cookies to improve your experience, analyse site traffic, and measure our ad performance. Essential cookies are always active. You can choose which optional cookies to accept."

7 Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in the cookies we use or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The effective date at the top will always reflect the most recent version.
